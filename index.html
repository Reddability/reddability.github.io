Ever felt as if reddit needed a facelift so it was more readable and easier to seperate posts, comments and messages? I did, that's why I created Reddability. It's a free Google Chrome and Safari extension that helps you discover reddit by giving it a little facelift. At the same time it keeps those things you love about reddit's simple design.

For those who'd like to share the experience of Reddability, there also is a subreddit stylesheet available with every release of Reddability. Download the zip-file, upload the images and copy-paste the CSS and you just put it on your subreddit! Please spread the word about Reddability by giving some credit. You can copy the markdown to the left and paste it in the sidebar or come up with your own!
