Ever felt as if reddit needed a facelift to make it more readable and easier to separate posts, comments and messages? I did, and that's why I created Reddability. It's a free Google Chrome extension that helps you discover reddit by giving it a facelift. At the same time it keeps the things you love about reddit's simple design. for those who'd like to share the experience of Reddability, there's also a subreddit stylesheet available with every release of Reddability. Download the zip-file, upload the images and copy-paste the CSS to enable it on your subreddit! Please spread the word about Readability by giving some credit.
